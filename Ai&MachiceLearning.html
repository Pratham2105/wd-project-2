About the Course

In this course, you will delve into the world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. All the videos will be uploaded on my YouTube channel “CodeWithHarry” under the playlist section, which you can access from the video description on YouTube. This course is designed for beginners as well as those looking to deepen their understanding of these cutting-edge technologies.

You will learn Python, a key programming language in AI and ML, along with libraries such as TensorFlow and scikit-learn. We will cover essential concepts like supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and more.

As you progress, you will work on real-world projects that will enhance your practical skills and understanding of machine learning algorithms and data analysis.

What You Will Learn

  • Introduction to Python for AI
  • Fundamentals of Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning with TensorFlow
  • Data Preprocessing and Analysis
  • Building and Training Models
  • Real-world Applications of AI
