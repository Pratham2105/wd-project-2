About the Course

In this course, you will explore the critical aspects of Cyber Security. All the videos will be uploaded on my YouTube channel “CodeWithHarry” under the playlist section, which you can access from the video description on YouTube. This course is tailored for beginners as well as those looking to enhance their knowledge in the field of cybersecurity.

You will learn about network security, ethical hacking, security protocols, and how to protect sensitive information from cyber threats. The course will cover both theoretical concepts and practical skills needed to secure networks and systems.

As you progress, you will work on real-world scenarios and projects to solidify your understanding of Cyber Security practices.

What You Will Learn

  • Introduction to Cyber Security
  • Network Security Fundamentals
  • Ethical Hacking Techniques
  • Security Best Practices
  • Incident Response and Management
  • Real-world Applications of Cyber Security
